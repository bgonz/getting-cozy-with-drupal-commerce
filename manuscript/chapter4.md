#Products & Product Reference
If there is one thing that identifies Commerce apart from other open source ecommerce systems it is how it handles products. Commerce brings to the virtual world the way we handle products in the physical world. A product isn't just an item with multiple attributes, a product is a collection of variations.

The Commerce product module provides the product entity, hence the module name.
